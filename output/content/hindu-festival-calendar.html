2026 Celebrations

Year of Devotion & Joy

A guide to the major auspicious days and festivals of 2026.

January: The Harvest

  • 13 Jan

    Lohri

    Bonfires and harvest celebration in Punjab.

  • 14 Jan

    Makar Sankranti / Pongal

    Sun enters Capricorn. Uttarayan begins.

  • 23 Jan

    Vasant Panchami

    Worship of Goddess Saraswati. Start of Spring preparations.

Spring: Colors of Faith

  • 15 Feb

    Maha Shivaratri

    The Great Night of Lord Shiva.

  • 03 Mar

    Holika Dahan

    Burning of the demoness Holika.

  • 04 Mar

    Holi

    Festival of Colors.

April: New Years

  • 19 Mar

    Ugadi / Gudi Padwa

    Kannada, Telugu & Marathi New Year.

  • 27 Mar

    Ram Navami

    Birth of Lord Rama.

  • 14 Apr

    Puthandu / Baisakhi

    Tamil & Sikh New Year.

Monsoon Festivals

  • 17 Jul

    Rath Yatra

    Chariot Festival of Jagannath Puri.

  • 28 Aug

    Raksha Bandhan

    Celebrating the bond of brother and sister.

Autumn: Divine Victory

  • 04 Sep

    Janmashtami

    Birth of Lord Krishna.

  • 14 Sep

    Ganesh Chaturthi

    Arrival of Lord Ganesha.

  • 20 Oct

    Dussehra (Vijayadashami)

    Victory of Good over Evil.

November: Lights

  • 06 Nov

    Dhanteras

    Worship of wealth and health.

  • 08 Nov

    Diwali (Deepavali)

    The Festival of Lights.

  • 19 Nov

    Dev Uthani Ekadashi

    End of Chaturmas, start of wedding season.
