FAQs

Uttarayan kab hai (2026)?

Uttarayan (Makar Sankranti) in 2026 is on Jan 14, 2026 (Wednesday).

Is Uttarayan the same as Makar Sankranti?

Uttarayan is commonly used as the festival name (especially in Gujarat) for Makar Sankranti celebrations. Both relate to the sun’s movement and mid-January festivities.

Why do muhurat timings differ?

Muhurat depends on local sunrise/sunset and regional panchang calculations, so it can vary by city. Use your local panchang for the most accurate timings.
