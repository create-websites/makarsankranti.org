Traditions & city highlights — including Ahmedabad Uttarayan and Surat Uttarayan.

Why Uttarayan is so famous in Gujarat

The Uttarayan festival Gujarat celebration is known for rooftop kite flying, community gatherings, and winter foods shared with neighbors and family. In many areas, you’ll hear celebrations start early in the morning and continue with food, music, and friendly competition.

Kites

Terraces fill up and skies turn colorful with patang and reels.

Food

Til–gud sweets and winter specials are shared as a sign of goodwill.

Community

Neighbors, families, and friends celebrate together across rooftops and streets.

Traditions of Uttarayan festival in Gujarat

Morning start

Many people start early to enjoy stable morning wind and sunlight.

Friendly kite duels

Competitive flying is common—keep it friendly, safe, and within local rules.

Festival food

Winter snacks and sweets are shared on terraces throughout the day.

Safety first

Use safe string, fly in open areas, and protect kids, riders, and birds.

Ahmedabad Uttarayan

Ahmedabad Uttarayan is one of the most searched experiences—known for terrace-to-terrace kite flying, all-day celebrations, and buzzing neighborhoods. Visitors often look for open grounds, city events, and markets for kites and reels.

What to plan for

  • Buy kites and safer string early (seasonal demand spikes).
  • Choose an open area or terrace with safe boundaries.
  • Keep gloves + first-aid ready for string cuts.

Surat Uttarayan

Surat Uttarayan is also highly searched. People commonly celebrate with kite flying, family gatherings, and terrace food spreads. Planning for safe flying spaces and safer string options is key.

Kite focus

Terraces and open spaces become the main celebration zones.

Food focus

Winter snacks and sweets create the warm, shared festival feeling.

Vadodara & Rajkot (more Gujarat cities)

Along with Ahmedabad and Surat, many people also celebrate across Vadodara and Rajkot with kite flying, community meals, and local markets for kites and accessories.
